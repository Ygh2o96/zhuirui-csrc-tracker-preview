口径说明 Methodology

默认页为上市申请中；已上市页纳入HKEX New Listing Report显示上市日不早于2023-03-31的全量发行人。申请中页A1至接收统计纳入日期齐全且顺序有效样本；如历史A1锚点至接收超过180个自然日，统计卡改用当前A1至当前接收，若当前A1后尚无接收日则剔除并打「outlier剔除统计」标签。已上市页展示首次A1至备案通过、首次A1至上市两组天数及上市日总市值（首日收盘×现行总股本，yfinance口径）；无需备案发行人不纳入备案通过统计。备案要求按周期判断：只要任一次A1递交日不早于2023-03-31即需备案，仅全部A1周期早于该日的发行人标记「制度前A1」。左侧「搁置/撤回」为实操口径：港交所失效/撤回但证监会备案已被接收的发行人大概率将重新递交，仍归入上市申请中并打「A1已失效」标签；仅 (a) 最近A1超过6个月且备案始终未获接收、或 (b) 已接收但停留征求意见且最近A1超过一年未更新 的发行人归入搁置/撤回。页面天数统一向上取整。

Default view tracks live applications; the Listed view covers all HKEX New Listing Report issuers listed on or after 2023-03-31. A1→received statistics include records with complete, chronologically valid dates; where the historical anchor exceeds 180 calendar days the cards switch to the current-cycle A1 (records without a current-cycle receipt are excluded and tagged "outlier"). The Listed view shows first-A1→notice and first-A1→listing durations plus listing-day market cap (first close × current shares outstanding, via yfinance). Filing applicability is cycle-based: any A1 submitted on or after 2023-03-31 triggers the CSRC filing requirement; only issuers whose every A1 cycle predates that date are tagged "Pre-regime A1". The "Stalled / withdrawn" bucket is practitioner-defined: lapsed HKEX applications with a live CSRC filing typically refile and stay under "In application" (tagged "A1 lapsed"); only (a) no CSRC receipt with the latest A1 older than 6 months, or (b) stuck at regulator-opinion with no new A1 for over a year, are parked here. Day counts round up.
